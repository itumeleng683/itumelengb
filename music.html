Basotho Music & Dance

Music and dance are central to Basotho cultural life, performed at weddings, initiations, and community celebrations.

Famo Music

Famo is a popular Basotho music style played with the accordion and often accompanied by dancing.

Mohobelo Dance

The Mohobelo is a traditional male dance known for strong stomping movements performed in unison.

1. Ndlamo Dance

The Ndlamo is a vibrant celebratory dance performed at weddings and community events, often accompanied by drums and singing.

2. Mokhibo Dance

A dance performed by women, characterized by kneeling movements and graceful hand gestures.

3. Lekope

A traditional wind instrument made from animal horn, used in ceremonies and dances.

4. Koriana

A key instrument in Famo music, producing lively melodies that accompany singing and dance.

5. Mangae

Traditional songs sung during initiation ceremonies, teaching values and history.

6. Lesiba

A stringed wind instrument unique to the Basotho, producing a humming sound.

7. Setolo-tolo

A mouth harp-like instrument producing rhythmic sounds, often played solo.

8. Mokorotlo Song

A traditional song associated with national pride, often sung at ceremonies.

9. Sehlako Dance

A communal dance involving rhythmic clapping and chanting.

10. Moribo

A genre of traditional songs performed by groups, often with call-and-response style.

11. Lekolulo

A flute-like instrument traditionally played by young herdboys in the fields.

12. Pina tsa Lenyalo

Wedding songs sung to bless and celebrate the couple.

13. Mohobelo

Different regional variations of the Mohobelo dance, each with unique steps.

14. Kora

A string instrument sometimes used in Basotho music, borrowed from neighboring cultures.

15. Moropa

Moropa are traditional Basotho drums, widely used during ceremonies, songs, and dances.

16. Mokorotlo Dance

A ceremonial dance performed to honor chiefs and national identity.

17. Famo Dance

The dance style that accompanies Famo music, with energetic movements and stomping.

18. Litolobonya

A form of song and dance performed exclusively by women and girls.
